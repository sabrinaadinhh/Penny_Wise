The budgeting and savings app that encourages users to not spend and get one step closer to their financial goals

With multiple meme notifications to remind users not to spend

  • Customize your savings! Set up as many savings goals as you would like for different occasions! Set a due date for each goal to remind yourself to work towards each goal by a set date.

  • Customize your budget! Input your monthly income, personal expenses, transportation fees, etc. and then let the app calculate how much you spend monthly!

  • Link all of your bank accounts all in one app! Monitor each bank account at your own leisure!

  • Customize your own notifications! You can pick the frequency of notification or which alerts you would like to be notified for!
